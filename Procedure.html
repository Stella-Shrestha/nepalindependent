Entry Procedure to Nepal

To visit Nepal for mountaineering and trekking, you need a valid passport with at least six months of validity from the visit date. Depending on your nationality, you may require a visa. Either a visa upon arrival at the Tribhuvan International Airport in Kathmandu or at several land border crossings. However, please check the visa requirements beforehand as they can vary. Additionally, travelers are required to fill out a customs declaration form upon arrival. Make sure to comply with any health requirements, especially in light of any ongoing health concerns or pandemics. It’s always wise to double-check the entry requirements before your trip to ensure a smooth arrival process. There are two ways to enter Nepal, by air and by road. The air journey is easier and comfortable than by road. Almost 90% of the tourists enter through the Tribhuvan International Airport, which is 5 miles or 8 kilometers from the heart of Kathmandu.

01

By Air

The major international airlines that operate scheduled services to Nepal are Royal Nepal Airlines Corporation(NAC), Aeroflot Russian Airlines, British Airways, Biman Bangladesh, China South West Airlines, Druk Air Bhutan, Gulf Air, Indian Airlines, Lufthansa, Pakistan International Airlines, Singapore Airlines, Thai Airways International, Transavia Airlines, and Qatar Airlines.

02

By Road

There are several entry points by land route. Among many entry points, Birganj, Belahia, and Kakarvita are popular entry points at the India-Nepal border, while Kodari’s entry point is at the Tibet ( China )- Nepal border.

03

Entry / Exit Points in Nepal

The entry and exit points for foreigners entering and departing from Nepal. The Immigration offices at such points are open 24 hours. Entering and departing from other places except the point is treated as a violation of the Immigration Act and the Regulation.

  • Immigration Office, Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu
  • Immigration Office, Kakarvitta, Jhapa, Eastern Nepal
  • Immigration Office, Birganj, Parsa, Central Nepal
  • Immigration Office, Kodari, Sindhupalchowk, Northern Border
  • Immigration Office, Belahia, Bhairahawa, Rupandehi, Western Nepal
  • Immigration Office, Jamunaha, Nepalgunj, Banke, Mid Western Nepal
  • Immigration Office, Mohana, Dhangadhi, Kailali, Far Western Nepal
  • Immigration Office, Gaddachauki, Mahendranagar, Far Western Nepal
  • Immigration Office, Pokhara, not an entry point
