Passport Copy: You need to provide a clear copy of your passport. The passport must be valid for at least 6 months from the date of your trip. And a passport-size photo.

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel insurance before starting your trip to Nepal. Your insurance should cover emergency medical treatment, helicopter evacuation, trip cancellation, lost or delayed luggage, and other unexpected situations that may arise during your journey.

For reliable and widely used coverage options, you may consider the following insurance providers:

  1. Allianz Travel Insurance
  2. World Nomads
  3. Faye Travel Insurance
  4. Global Assistance Italy

Selecting a policy that includes high-altitude trekking and helicopter rescue is essential, especially for treks above 4,000 tyo 6000 meters.
